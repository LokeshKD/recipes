Ingredients:
------------

1. Tamarind.
2. Jeera
3. Salt.
4. Red Chilli
5. Curry leaves

Description:
------------

1. Make masala powder fist. crush jeera, salt, chilli first and then add curry leaves later for crushing.
2. Make sure the powder is grain less and very powdery.
3. Now batter tamarind for 4-5 mins adding masala powder for the next 5 mins.
4. Mouth watering Masala Tamarind is ready for consumption.
